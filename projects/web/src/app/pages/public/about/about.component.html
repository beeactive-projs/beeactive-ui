About Us

We've been going to the gym, running, playing sports — staying active — for as long as we can remember. BeeActive is the platform we always wished existed.

Why we're building this.

We're two developers who love staying active. Gym sessions, group runs, weekend hikes, martial arts — if it gets us moving, we're in. Over the years we've been part of dozens of groups, classes, and communities.

Every time, the same thing happened: WhatsApp groups for scheduling, spreadsheets for tracking who's coming, Instagram for updates, and half the group out of the loop anyway.

We thought — why isn't there one simple place where the person leading the activity and the people showing up can just stay in sync? No noise, no switching apps, no chaos.

So we started building BeeActive.

It's early. We're two people with a clear idea and the skills to build it. No VC money, no fake "10K members" stats. Just the thing we wish we had, being built in the open.

What we believe.

Community first.

The best motivation isn't an app notification — it's the people you show up with.

Keep it simple.

If it takes more than a minute to figure out, we built it wrong.

Not just fitness.

Yoga, hiking, martial arts, dance — if people come together to move, it belongs here.

Build in the open.

No fake metrics. We share what we're building, what's ready, and what's next.
