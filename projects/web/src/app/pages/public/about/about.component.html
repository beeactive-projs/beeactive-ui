Our Story

From Startup to Community

BeeActive was born from a simple frustration: quality personal training was only accessible to those in the right city, with the right budget, and the right connections.

Founded in 2020, we set out to change that. Today we connect over 10,000 active members with 500+ certified trainers worldwide — through a platform built entirely around results.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with the human touch of professional coaching to create a fitness experience that's both effective and deeply personal.
